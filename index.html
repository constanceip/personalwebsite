A Brief Background

I am a front-end web and graphic design enthusiast who works with WordPress, HTML/CSS, and Javascript/jQuery. I am also an intermediate user of Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

Much of my web and design knowledge was gained through trial and error (and frequent access to online videos), mostly while creating websites for friends and family. I am now completing a 4-month Web Development course with New York Code and Design Academy, which is bolstering my education in client-side languages and introducing me to server-side interactions.

Originally from San Francisco, I spent 2008 to 2014 traveling and working in Argentina, China, Singapore, and Hong Kong. During this time, I worked as first a teacher, then as a business development professional for management consulting companies, responsible for lead generation, relationship development, and sales. In 2013, I co-founded a coding and design academy in Hong Kong called BSD Academy.

I love travel blogging, photography, and gastro-tourism. I am now living in Philadelphia with my husband, Matt.
